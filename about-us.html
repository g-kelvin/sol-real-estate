About Sol Real Estate

You are Number one

At Sol Real Estate, you are number one. Whether you are a property owner, tenant or buyer, We value your business and we will provide you with individual attention and service you deserve. We believe a strict code of ethics. We believe in integrity, commitment to excellence, a professional attitude and professional care.

Sol provides a wide variety of real estate services to investors. We understand that you do not want to be in business of real estate and property management, but we do. Some of the solutions we offer to our clients are:

  • Marketing for sale
  • Property while vacant or occupied
  • Cleaning
  • General Maintenance services
  • Marketing property for lease
  • Short term / Long term Lease/ Air BnBs
